Frequently Asked Questions

Which social apps are you compatible with?

We're starting of with Discord, Telegram and Twitter. Feel free to tell us which one you'd love to see next!

Which dApps can I subscribe to events?

We aim to support as many Terra dApps as possible by EoY, and eventually become multi-chain native.

How much will it cost me?

Nought, nil, nada, nothing, zero, zilch and zip. The platform will be 100% free to retail (non-business) users.

Will you support SMS notifications? What about mobile apps?

Yes, at some point, but it's not a priority on our roadmap. We are also planning to release Android & iOS apps down the line, but probably not in 2022.

What about my Smart Home appliances? Lightbulbs, music player, etc.

By using popular integration services such as IFTTT, Zapier, Make and more, you'll have virtually infinite choices on how and where you want to be notified.

How are you "infinitely scalable" and instant everywhere?

Our website and webapps literally "live" on the cloud edge (also called CDN) of Cloudflare. We leverage the power of their thousands of servers worldwide (193 cities) by using their Pages + Worker services, and as an added bonus, we're DDoS protected too!
